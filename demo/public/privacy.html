Privacy Notice — Public Beta Demo

Applies only to the free public demo at this domain. Last updated 2026-07-14.

This page covers the public beta demo only — the version anyone can try with a one-time 6-digit code, no account required. It's a completely different deployment from Bede itself, which real families run entirely on their own hardware with nothing sent to us at all. If you're evaluating Bede for your own family's use, the self-hosted product's privacy model is stricter than everything below — see the main project's documentation for that.

In short: we never store the conversation itself. We do store, briefly, the name/grade you optionally provide and anonymized structural signals about how the session went (which tools were used, how many turns, which subjects) — never what was actually said. Feedback is entirely optional, and we never ask a child for their own email address.

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) — Parental Notice

bede™ ("we," "us," or "our") operates this online demo, which may be used by children under 13 with a parent or guardian present. Because a child may use this service, the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act ("COPPA") requires us to give you this notice and, where we collect personal information from your child, to obtain your consent first — which is why the demo's entry screen gates access behind the consent step described above before any of this is collected.

Information we collect from your child

This demo does not use accounts — there is no username, password, phone number, or payment information collected from your child at any point. What we collect, only with your consent, is limited to:

  • Name and grade level (optional) — used only to personalize the one session in progress, kept for at most 6 hours, and never built into a persistent profile.
  • The conversation itself, transmitted in real time to our AI provider, Anthropic, so Bede can generate its tutoring responses — never stored by us and never used by us or, per Anthropic's terms, by Anthropic to train models.
  • Anonymized interaction-pattern signals (which teaching tools were used, how many turns, which subjects) — cryptographically keyed so it cannot be traced back to your child, and never includes anything that was actually said.

We do not require your child to provide more information than is reasonably necessary to try the demo, and every field above is optional except the conversation itself, which is inherent to how a tutoring session works and is not retained afterward.

Information we collect only from a parent or guardian

At the end of a session, we show an optional "how could this be better?" prompt. A child is never asked for their own contact information. If a follow-up email address is provided, our interface requires you to first affirm that you are the parent or guardian before that field even appears, and the address is used once, to reply, and is never written to a database.

How we use this information

We use the information described above only to:

  • Personalize the single tutoring session in progress.
  • Generate Bede's tutoring responses (via Anthropic's Claude API).
  • Understand, in aggregate and anonymized form, which teaching patterns work well, so we can improve Bede.
  • Reply to a parent or guardian who has asked us to.

Disclosure to third parties

We do not sell your child's information, and we do not use it for advertising. We share it only with the service providers that make the demo work — Anthropic (to generate Bede's responses) and Resend (to deliver an optional feedback email) — each of whom is contractually and technically limited to using it for that purpose. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect someone's safety.

Your parental rights under COPPA

At any time, you may:

  • Ask what personal information we hold related to your child's session.
  • Ask us to delete it.
  • Withdraw your consent and end your child's session.

In practice there is very little to review or delete once a session ends — name/grade expire automatically within 6 hours and the conversation itself is never stored — but if you'd like to exercise these rights, or believe your child shared more than described here, contact us at info@agnusdei.ai with your child's session details and we'll act on it promptly.

What happens if you do not consent

If you do not agree at the consent step, your child will not be able to use this demo. Nothing else on this page requires consent beyond that single gate — there is no further, separate collection hidden inside the session itself.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, usernames, or passwords — there is nothing to create or remember.
  • No phone numbers and no payment information are ever collected in this demo.
  • No advertising trackers, no third-party analytics pixels, no ad networks.
  • We never sell your data or use it for advertising.
  • No persistent account or profile is created from a demo session — a session code is discarded (or expires) and that's the end of it.
